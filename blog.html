The Complete Guide to Roof Maintenance and Care

Proper roof maintenance is crucial for protecting your home and preserving property value. This comprehensive guide outlines essential practices, including bi-annual inspections focusing on shingles, flashing, and drainage systems. Homeowners should watch for warning signs like water stains, loose shingles, and sagging decks while implementing preventive measures such as gutter cleaning and tree trimming. Professional expertise is recommended for severe weather damage, water issues, and annual inspections, especially for roofs over 15 years old. Regular maintenance proves more cost-effective than major repairs, requiring detailed documentation, immediate minor repairs, and proper insurance coverage for optimal long-term roof protection.
